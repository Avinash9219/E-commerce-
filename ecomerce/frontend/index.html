FAQ

How does customization work?

Choose products, share your design/photo/message, and we confirm the final preview on WhatsApp before printing.

What is the delivery timeline?

Most orders ship within 24–48 hours. Express delivery depends on your city and pincode.

Can I change the hamper items after ordering?

Yes, as long as production has not started. Message us on WhatsApp with your order details.

Do you support bulk/corporate gifting?

Absolutely. We offer logo printing, custom packaging, and bulk pricing for corporate orders.
